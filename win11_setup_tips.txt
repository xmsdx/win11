Registry Key to Bypass CPU Check:

When you reach the Windows 11 setup screen:
1) Press Shift + F10 to open Command Prompt.
2) Type regedit and press Enter to open the Registry Editor.
3) HKEY_LOCAL_MACHINE\SYSTEM\Setup\LabConfig
	3a) If LabConfig does not exist, create it under Setup.
4) Bypass CPU Check
	4a) Add a new DWORD (32-bit) value:
	4b) Name: BypassCPUCheck
	4c) Value: 1
5) To bypass SecureBoot (optional)
	5a) Add a new DWORD (32-bit) value:
	5b) Name: BypassSecureBootCheck
	5c) Value: 1
